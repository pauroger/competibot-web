    What competibot does

    • 🎯 Skill tracking for any activity
    • 🧩 Works where you are – Slack and Discord
    • 🏆 Unlimited rankings – one per Slack or Discord channel (and DMs where supported)
    • ⚔️ Match types include:

    🥇 Head-to-head

    • /1v1 winner loser – Classic duel
    • /1v1_draw player player – Call it a draw 🤝

    👫 Team Battles

    • /2v2 winner winner loser loser – Doubles match
    • /2v2_draw player_team1 player_team1 player_team2 player_team2 – Declare a team draw
    • /3v3 winner winner winner loser loser loser – Triple threat teams
    • /3v3_draw player_team1 player_team1 player_team1 player_team2 player_team2 player_team2 – Triple draw 🔁
    • /4v4 winner winner winner winner loser loser loser loser – 8-player team clash
    • /4v4_draw player_team1 player_team1 player_team1 player_team1 player_team2 player_team2 player_team2 player_team2 – Draw among giants 💥

    🎉 Free-For-Alls (FFA)

    • /ffa_3 first second third – 3-player rumble
    • /ffa_4 first second third fourth – 4-way chaos
    • /ffa_5 through /ffa_8 – Up to 8 players, ranked from winner to last place!
